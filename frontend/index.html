What is GiveFund?

Why does this exist?

Most donors only see campaigns in one app or one feed. GiveFund aggregates public listings so you can find people to help before the moment passes.

Which platforms do you support?

Twelve sources including GoFundMe, LaunchGood, JustGiving, Givebutter, GlobalGiving, Patreon, and more — with new discover scrapers added over time.

Do you handle campaign payments?

No. “Give now” always opens the original fundraiser on their platform. GiveFund never holds or routes money for campaigns.

What is Stripe Link?

Stripe Link is a secure wallet that saves your card with Stripe (not GiveFund). If you enroll via our optional checkout, use the same email when you donate on GoFundMe, Givebutter, Donorbox, or other Link-enabled checkouts — you may see Link and skip re-entering your card. LaunchGood and some GoFundMe paths do not support Link.

Why the optional tip?

It keeps discovery free and creates a real Stripe checkout so Link can save your payment details. You can choose $1 if you only want Link enrollment.
