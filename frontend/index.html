What is GiveFund?

Why does this exist?

Most donors only see campaigns in one app or one feed. GiveFund aggregates public listings so you can find people to help before the moment passes.

Which platforms do you support?

Twelve sources including GoFundMe, LaunchGood, JustGiving, Givebutter, GlobalGiving, Patreon, and more — with new discover scrapers added over time.

Do you handle campaign payments?

No. “Give now” always opens the original fundraiser on their platform. GiveFund never holds or routes money for campaigns — and we never ask for tips or donations to GiveFund.

Is GiveFund really free?

Yes. There are no accounts, no paywalls, and no payments to us. We’re a discovery layer; your gift goes entirely through the platform hosting the fundraiser.

Can GiveFund pay with my saved card?

No. We can’t pass payment tokens to GoFundMe, LaunchGood, or other hosts. Optional card save is only for Stripe Link on sites that support it — you always finish checkout on the original platform.
