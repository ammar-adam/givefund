Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  Questions? privacy@givefund.app

What GiveFund is

GiveFund is a search tool that surfaces crowdfunding campaigns from public platforms. We do not run campaigns, collect donations, or hold any funds.

Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to run the optional wallet feature:

  • Google account email and display name — when you choose to sign in with Google. We verify your identity via Google's OAuth 2.0 service.
  • Stripe customer ID — created by Stripe when you save a payment method. GiveFund never sees your full card number; Stripe handles all card data under PCI-DSS.
  • Session tokens — HMAC-signed, stored in your browser's sessionStorage only (cleared when you close the tab). Never stored server-side.

We do not collect browsing history, track which campaigns you view, set third-party tracking cookies, or sell any data.

How we use your information

  • To prefill your name and email on supported donation checkouts, saving you steps.
  • To associate your Stripe customer record so Stripe Link can autofill payment details.

Data storage and security

Donor profiles (email, Stripe customer ID, display name) are stored in a SQLite database on Render's infrastructure in the United States. The database is not included in public releases — donor data is excluded from the open-source repository.

All connections use HTTPS/TLS. The API enforces rate limiting and requires authentication for all wallet operations.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In — governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — payment method storage governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Render — API hosting.
  • Vercel — frontend hosting.

Your rights

You can delete all your wallet data at any time from the Wallet page using the "Delete my wallet data" button. This removes your profile from our database immediately. Your Stripe payment method must be deleted separately via Stripe's dashboard.

Children

GiveFund is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We will post updates here with a revised date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
